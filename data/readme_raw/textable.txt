This package aims to replicate functionality from the R package xtable . It currently supports the creation of LaTeX tables from simple arrays and matrices. Quickstart using TeXTable; A = [ 1 2 3 ; 4 5 6 ; 7 8 9 ]; Table (A) This results in \begin{table}[!h] \centering \begin{tabular}{lll} 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 4 & 5 & 6 \\ 7 & 8 & 9 \\ \end{tabular} \end{table} More options for table can be found from ?
