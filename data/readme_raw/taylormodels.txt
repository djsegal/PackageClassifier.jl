TaylorModels This package combines the IntervalArithmetic.jl and TaylorSeries.jl packages to provide Taylor models , i.e. Taylor polynomials with guaranteed error bounds to approximate functions. An introduction is available in this video from JuliaCon 2018 . Authors Luis Benet , Instituto de Ciencias Físicas, Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM) David P.
