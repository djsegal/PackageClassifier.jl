Clipper Clipper.jl is a Julia wrapper for Angus Johnson's Clipper library (ver. 6.2.1). . Clipper - an open source freeware library for clipping and offsetting lines and polygons. The Clipper library performs line & polygon clipping - intersection, union, difference & exclusive-or, and line & polygon offsetting. The library is based on Vatti's clipping algorithm.
