RestrictProlong This package provides efficient multidimensional implementations of two operators, restrict and prolong , which feature heavily in multigrid methods. In general terms, these operations reduce and increase, respectively, the size of arrays by a factor of 2 along one or more dimensions. The two operators satisfy the "Galerkin condition," meaning that as operators they are transposes of one another. In addition to being useful for mulitigrid methods, restrict can be used as a fast antialiasing thumbnail generator for images.
