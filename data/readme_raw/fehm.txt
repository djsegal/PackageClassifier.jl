FEHM.jl (Julia interface for FEHM) FEHM.jl interfaces FEHM . FEHM.jl is a module of the ZEM framework. ZEM is an integrated open-source high-performance computational (HPC) framework for environmental management in Julia . ZEM includes a series of modules (FEHM.jl, mysql.jl, etc.). ZEM is coupled with Mads . ZEM has been tested to perform HPC simulations on a wide-range multi-processor clusters and parallel environments (Moab, Slurm, etc.).
