ImplicitPlots.jl Plot plane curves and surfaces defined by a function f(x,y)=0 resp. f(x,y,z)=0 . Plane curves using ImplicitPlots f (x,y) = (x ^ 4 + y ^ 4 - 1 ) * (x ^ 2 + y ^ 2 - 2 ) + x ^ 5 * y implicit_plot (f; xlims = ( - 2 , 2 ), ylims = ( - 2 , 2 )) Polynomials following the MultivariatePolynomials.jl interface are also supported. For convenience also the @polyvar macro from DynamicPolynomials.jl is exported. @polyvar x y f2 = (x ^ 4 + y ^ 4 - 1 ) * (x ^ 2 + y ^ 2 - 2 ) + x ^ 5 * y implicit_plot (f2; xlims = ( - 2 , 2 ), ylims = ( - 2 , 2 )) Surface For surfaces you need to have a backend for AbstractPlotting installed. using ImplicitPlots using Makie # GLMakie backend for AbstractPlotting @polyvar x y z g = ( 0.3 * x ^ 2 + 0.5 z - 0.3 x + 1.2 * y ^ 2 - 1.1 ) ^ 2 + ( 0.7 * (y + 0.5 x) ^ 2 + y + 1.2 * z ^ 2 - 1 ) ^ 2 - 0.3 implicit_plot (g)
