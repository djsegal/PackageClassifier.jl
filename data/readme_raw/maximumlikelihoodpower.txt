KSstatistic KSstatistic (data :: AbstractVector , alpha) --> Float64 Return the Kolmogorov-Smirnov statistic comparing data to a power law with power alpha . The elements of data are assumed to be unique. Minimizing the KS statistic over alpha is another way to estimate the parameter of the sample distribution.
