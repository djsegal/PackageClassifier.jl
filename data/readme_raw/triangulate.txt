Triangulate Julia wrapper for Johnathan Richard Shewchuk's Triangle mesh generator. The package tries to provide a 1:1 mapping of Triangle's functionality to Julia. Useful information about Triangle: Triangle home page https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~quake/triangle.html with instructions for using Triangle Research credit, references, and online papers Troubleshooting Command line switches This package uses ideas from TriangleMesh.jl and Triangle.jl .
