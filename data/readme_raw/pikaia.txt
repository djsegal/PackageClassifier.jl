Pikaia.jl [ https://gitter.im/tmeits/pikaia.jl ] Welcome to the Pikaia.jl homepage. [ http://tmeits.github.io/pikaia.jl ] Pikaia (pronounced "Pee-kah-yah") is a general purpose function optimization Julia Lang module based on a genetic algorithm. Source An Pikaia.jl transliteration of the original FORTRAN-77 has been produced by Sarah Gibson, and is available directly by http://download.hao.ucar.edu/archive/pikaia/pikaia.f .
