EnglishText Currently this package includes six features: indefinite for finding the right indefinite article to use english for converting a positive integer to an English-language wordy representation, and the inverse function unenglish to convert the wordy representation back. pluralize to pluralize a noun, and the inverse function singularize ItemList to create a list of objects ItemQuantity to associate a noun with a number sentences to iterate over the sentences of a text See the documentation (links above) for how to use these features.
